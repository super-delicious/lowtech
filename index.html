Social Distance Shelter

Yixin Cao


My inspiration originates from the fact that the epidemic is a contagious disease that requires people to keep a social distance. This motivates me to create a virtual space as the scenery of spiritual healing for security purposes. It is also the revelation of the reality of environmental degradation. I constructed a shelter to keep individuals in a comfortable and safe environment, reducing their exposure to dangerous outside conditions.
View in Low Tech

The aim is to reduce energy usage by using minimal web design. To reduce energy consumption substantially below that of a typical website, use default fonts and static graphics.
